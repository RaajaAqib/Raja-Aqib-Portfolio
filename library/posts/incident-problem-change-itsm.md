

## Overview

ITSM defines structured processes to restore services, identify root causes, and apply controlled changes.

---

## Incident Management

An **Incident** represents an unplanned interruption or service degradation.

:::info
The goal of Incident Management is **service restoration**, not root cause analysis.
:::

---

## Problem Management

A **Problem** focuses on identifying the **underlying cause** of one or more incidents.

:::tip
Use Problem records when incidents repeat or have unknown root causes.
:::

---

## Change Management

A **Change** ensures controlled modifications to services or infrastructure.

:::warning
Never bypass Change Management for production updates.
:::

---

## Key Differences

| Process | Purpose |
|------|------|
| Incident |    Restore service |
| Problem |     Remove root cause |
| Change |      Implement controlled fix |
