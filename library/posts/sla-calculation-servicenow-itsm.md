

## Overview

SLAs define response and resolution expectations and are evaluated automatically.

---

## SLA Lifecycle

## Start Condition
Triggered when a record meets SLA criteria.

## Pause Condition
Timer pauses when defined conditions are met.

## Resume Condition
Timer resumes when pause conditions clear.

## Stop Condition
SLA completes when resolution criteria are satisfied.

:::important
Incorrect pause conditions are the most common SLA misconfiguration.
:::
